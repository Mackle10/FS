About FELLOWSHIP PRIMARY SCHOOL

FELLOWSHIP PRIMARY SCHOOL — DAY & BOARDING - LUGAZI

P.O Box 276 Lugazi

Fellowship Primary School (Day & Boarding)-Lugazi is a Christian founded school, mixed day and boarding for both Pre-Primary and Primary sections. The school is committed to quality education, Christian discipline, high morals, hardwork and a conducive environment.

Overview

Our primary program focuses on literacy, numeracy, science, and socials with child-centered learning.

Facilities & Fees

  • Fees: Affordable and friendly rates.
  • A school with a well designed compound.
  • Well aerated spacious dormitories.
  • Conducive learning environment.
  • Well trained and caring staff.

Curriculum Highlights

  • English language and reading
  • Mathematics fundamentals
  • Basic science and exploration
  • Creative arts and physical education
